Dr. Gary Fettke

He chose his patients over his medical board and won.

As an orthopedic surgeon in Tasmania, Australia, Dr. Gary Fettke grew tired of amputating the limbs of diabetic patients while the hospital force-fed them sugary food. He began advocating for Low-Carbohydrate, High-Fat (LCHF) diets to actively save their limbs.

In response, he was systematically silenced and dragged through hell by local dietetic authorities. Along with his wife Belinda, he fought a multi-year legal battle against the establishment, eventually securing a complete exoneration and a formal apology. He remains an absolute titan of medical integrity.
